Our Services

MINOR Severity

The 'Minor Severity' option is for mild symptoms or non-urgent conditions. It provides convenient access to telemedicine services for personalized medical advice and treatment recommendations without needing to visit a healthcare facility. This option streamlines healthcare processes, offering timely assistance while reducing strain on resources.

Select MINOR Severity

INTERMEDIATE Severity

The 'Intermediate Severity' option is chosen when medical attention is needed for conditions that are not life-threatening but require evaluation by a healthcare professional. It allows access to a wider range of healthcare services, including in-person consultations at partner hospitals. Patients can schedule appointments based on availability and receive tailored care. This option ensures continuity of care by coordinating telemedicine consultations with in-person visits, providing a seamless healthcare experience.

Select INTERMEDIATE Severity

SEVERE Severity

The 'Severe Severity' option provides immediate access to emergency services and offers the convenience of scheduling a home appointment for treatment. Ideal for critical situations, it ensures prompt medical intervention tailored to the patient's needs, even when traveling to a healthcare facility is challenging.

Select SEVERE Severity
